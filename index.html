Documentation & Tutorial

How to use the App

  1. Upload your PDF: Click "Choose File" at the top right to select the PDF you want to impose.
  2. Select Imposition Mode: Use the first tab in the sidebar to select your desired layout (e.g., Saddle Stitch for booklets, Multi-out for business cards).
  3. Adjust Paper Size: Make sure the paper width and height match the physical paper you are printing on.
  4. Set Bleeds & Margins: Configure the necessary trim bleeds and margins based on your printer's specifications.
  5. Preview: Click the orange "Preview Imposition" button to render the result.
  6. Download: Once satisfied, click "Download PDF" to save the print-ready file to your computer.
